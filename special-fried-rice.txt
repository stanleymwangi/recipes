Ingredients:
 - 3 cups cooked rice
 - 1/4 cup minced spring onion, sliced thinly
 - 1/4 cup Red Bell Pepper
 - 1/2 cup Green peas
 - 1/4 cup Green Beans
 - 1/4 cup Carrots
 - 1/4 cup Cabbage
 - 1/4 cup Celery
 - 2 tbsp Soy Sauce (or Tamari), or to taste
 - 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
 - 1 tbsp Garlic, minced
 - 1 tbsp Ginger, peeled and minced 
 
Directions:
 1. Heat a wok using high heat
 2. Add the ginger, garlic and green onion along with the vegetable oil
 3. Stir for 5-6 seconds then add all the rest of the vegetables when
    aroma starts wafting
 4. After 30-40 seconds, add the rice 
 5. Stir until well mixed
 6. Add soy sauce and mix well
 7. Celebrate by eating your fine meal
